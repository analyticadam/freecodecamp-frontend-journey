Hello Dolly Bars

Are you ready for your tastebuds to be blown away?

Ingredients

  • 1 Stick of Land O Lakes unsalted Butter (1/2 c. butter)
  • 1 cup of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup of shredded coconut
  • 1 cup of butterscotch chips (6 oz)
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips (6 oz)
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans
  • 1 can of Eagle Brand codensed milk (15 oz.)-Regular or Fat-Free

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Melt butter in the microwave for 30 second intervals until melted.
  3. Once melted, spread melted butter in a 9 x 13 inch pan.
  4. Sprinkle in the crushed graham cracker crumbs.
  5. Layer the shredded coconut, butterscotch chips, and chocolate chips and chopped pecans in alternating layers until all used.
  6. Pour the can of Eagle Brand condensed milk over the entire mixture in the pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
